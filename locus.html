Travel Time Maps

Locus helps you create custom travel time maps for the Swiss public transport network. Starting from a position of interest, such maps depict visually regions which are reachable by public transport within a certain time range.

Try it!Contact us→

Search any location in Switzerland

You can create a travel time maps for any location in Switzerland, simply type the address in the search box! Locus will soon be available for more countries.

Near real-time data

Transport times are computed using public transportation schedules published every week by the Swiss Federal Railways (SBB).

Multi-modal routes

Locus supports multi-modal routes combining various public transport modes: trains, trams, buses, cable cars, ferries...

Fast routing and map generation

Thanks to our efficient custom routing algorithm, complex travel time maps take only a few seconds to be generated.

Curated graphical interface

Our maps are dynamic, performant, and visually pleasing. The graphical interface and colors are specially designed to avoid visual clutter.

API calls & Embeddable maps

Professionals and power users can also generate maps via API calls and embed them on their own website.

Concept

Mobility is at the heart of our lifes. We use public transport to commute, meet with friends, visit new places and so much more.

We like to think that travel time is propotional to the distance of the trip. Unfortunately, this is not always true due to inefficiencies in public transport networks!

Locus helps assess better travel time: in a few seconds you can create your own personal map and visualize the areas easily reachable from a given location.

Read an isochrone map

After having selected your starting location (for instance your office or home), you will visualize different areas of colors on the map. Each color delimits an isochrone, i.e. all the locations that can be reached within the same time range (for instance 15-30 minutes). You may observe that there some easily reachable areas (in green) are much further away than you would expect (and respectively).

What can you do with isochrone maps?

Real Estate

You are looking for a new place but want to avoid too long commute time. Locus can help you for that! Generate a map centered around your office, determine which areas are easily reachable and focus you research there.

Tourism

You are going on a trip to Zürich. You want to be near the city center while not paying too much your hotel room. If you generate a map centered around your place of interest you will easily understand which areas of the city can offer you the tradeoff you are looking for.

Businesses

Isochrone maps have a great potential and endless applications. Contact us if you want to embed our maps in your website or if you need a bespoke analysis of the accessibility of your business by your customers or employees.
